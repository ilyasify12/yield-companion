Why you'll love it

Everything runs locally. Your voice never leaves your machine.

Voice AI

Natural conversations with a powerful AI model. Ask questions, get answers, dictate — all hands-free.

Desktop Control

Open applications, manage windows, play music, search files — your voice becomes your mouse and keyboard.

Wake Word

Say "Mia" or "James" and it's ready. No buttons, no hotkeys — it listens when you need it, ignores when you don't.

Lightweight
